About

A small studio with a serious approach to security.

Ayric Technologies was founded by a cybersecurity engineer with a simple conviction: that everyday people deserve software built with the same care and rigour usually reserved for the enterprise.

We work at the intersection of security and usability — applying real cryptographic engineering to problems that matter, then wrapping it in interfaces anyone can use. We build for our own community first, and to a standard we'd stake our name on.
